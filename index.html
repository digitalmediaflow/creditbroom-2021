We do hold your credit card on file to be charge monthly after we start working on your case. So you are not charged upfront for us to start working on your credit file. We do both credit repair and credit building. For credit repair. We work through you with the credit bureaus to remove destructive credit items. Every time we work with the credit bureaus, it takes 30 - 40 days to get an answer. We dont just wait for their response, like all these other so called credit repair agencies. How long does it take for me to get strong credit, like a 700 score or better?

It all depends on your current credit score and history. There are also some key factors such as late payments, debt/income ratios, income, aged accounts, and various delinquencies that affect your score. We are better than other companies that repair credit in that we usually have clients very happy with their scores between 4-8 months. However, everyone's credit is different.
